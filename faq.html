Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmartTools Hub really free?

Yes! Every tool on our platform is 100% free to use with no hidden costs or limits.

Are my files safe?

Absolutely. Unlike other online tools, we do NOT upload your files to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser's memory. Once you close the tab, everything is gone.

Do I need to create an account?

No registration is required. You can start using our tools immediately.

What happens if I have a large file?

Since the processing happens in your browser, the speed and file size limit depend on your device's RAM. Most modern browsers can handle files up to several hundred megabytes without issue.
