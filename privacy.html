Privacy Policy

At SmartTools Hub, accessible from smarttoolshub.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SmartTools Hub and how we use it.

Browser-Based Processing

SmartTools Hub is unique because it uses **client-side processing**. This means that when you use our PDF tools, Image resizers, or converters, your files are processed entirely within your web browser. **Your documents are never uploaded to our servers.**

Log Files

SmartTools Hub follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies

We use cookies only for essential site functions, such as remembering your theme preference (Dark/Light mode). We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
